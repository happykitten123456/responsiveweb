Brightstone Ragdoll Cattery

Brightstone Ragdoll Cattery is a small Ragdoll Cattery located in the heart of Nassau County on Long Island in New York. Just thirty minutes from New York City, Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, Staten Island and the Bronx.

We raise big, beautiful Ragdolls in our home and we love everything about them. From their sweet dispositions, deep throaty purrs, non matting soft and silky fur and gorgeous colors of seal and blue. All our cats are homebred from healthy genetic lines resulting in absolutely gorgeous, large, and breathtaking cats.

Rasied with Pride

All my Ragdoll Cats and Ragdoll Kittens are raised as part of my family, with love and pride, as well as underfoot. I even have built my Ragdolls their own room filled with all the cat toys and equipment they could ever want or need. I make sure that each and every one of my Ragdolls receives individual attention and special care. I enjoy my one on one time with them while I groom them, play catch, rub their bellies and let them sleep in my lap while I watch TV. My little Ragdoll Kittens are a pleasure to watch as they grow and play; especially as they learn to climb their scratching post and cat trees.

Why Brightstone

We have been a TICA and CFA Cattery of Excellence since 2003! I choose to become a Ragdoll breeder as well as Ragdoll showman because I truly love the Ragdoll breed. I started my Ragdoll Cattery with the knowledge that I wanted to show and breed healthy and solid lines of Ragdolls. My Ragdolls exhibit the larger size and personality that is the trade mark of Ragdoll Cats. My first Ragdoll Rhamee Blue, an alter, reached 20 pounds at maturity.

My Ragdoll breeding program is now sired by my own Regional Winner and Quadruple Grand Champion Brightstone Colton Jaxon Delamer the son of my own Outstanding Sire Regional Winner and Supreme Grand Champion Brightstone Baron Markee Delamer. These amazing boys have allowed me to reach my Ragdoll breeding goal of creating large Champion Ragdoll Cats. Additionally, I am focusing on breeding for genetic health and personality to ensure that the Ragdoll breed is kept within the TICA and CFA Association standards.

Brightstone Colton Jaxon Delamer

Jaxon has produced several amazing show cats. Stayed tuned as he creates a second ragdoll dynasty! Two of his newest offspring being shown won grand Championship.

Brightstone Baron Markee Delamer

Outstanding Sire is achieved when a male cat creates ten offspring that reach grand championship or higher award status. Markee is quailified for the outstanding sires.

Brightstone Family

My Ragdoll family can be found all over Long Island in Nassau County, Suffolk County, Manhattan, Queens, Staten Island, Brooklyn, Connecticut, Delaware, Maryland, Massachusetts, New Jersey, New York, North Carolina, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, Mississippi, Florida, and Virginia. We are also found internationally in Europe, Belgium, Latvia, Russia, China, and many other international countries. We do not ship pets but prefer a face to face choosing of your new family member. We always look forward to meeting you when you first visit to choose your kitten and when you come to pick up your new purring bundle of joy!

Cat Adoption Application Processes

Step 1:

Email (Brightstonerags8@aol.com) or Call (516 599-1708) David Nudleman if you are interested in reserving one of our kittens.

Step 2:

Go through an interview procedure.

Step 3:

A $250 nonrefundable deposit is required. It will either hold one of the available kittens or put you on our waiting list and allow you to get the next available kitten of your choice.

Step 4:

Select kittens you're interesting in adopting and schedule a time to meet them in person.

Step 5:

Go through this cat adoption checklist for essential cat supplies and prepare for their arrival.

Step 6:

Picking up your kittens. All kittens will be ready to go to their new homes between the ages of 12-14 weeks of age.
